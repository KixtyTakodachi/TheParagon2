Social responsibility

Paragon Group supports a number of corporate social responsibility programmes to enrich and improve the lives of the communities where we operate.

Our corporate social responsibility has several main directions:
Fair business practice: development of activities for the welfare of society, minimizing social and environmental business development costs, increasing workplace efficiency;
Team development: attracting highly qualified personnel, creating opportunities to realize potential through best practices, and investment in individual development;
Environmental protection: upholding the principles of a thoughtful and logical approach to preserving the natural world;
Encouragement of charity and sponsorship: implementing charitable programmes and encouraging personal contribution to good causes;
